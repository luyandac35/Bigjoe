Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to order?

No. Customers can browse the menu, add items to cart, checkout and place an order without logging in.

Who must log in?

Only the admin logs in. The admin can manage orders, products, inventory, promotions, customers and reports.

How do I collect my order?

After checkout, you receive a ticket number like BJ12345. Wait for staff to call your number, then collect your order.

Can I pay by card?

Yes, the checkout page has card fields. For this demo project, real bank payments are not processed and full card numbers are not saved.

How do I track my order?

Open Track Orders and search using your ticket number or the phone number used during checkout.

How long does preparation take?

Usually 10–20 minutes, depending on how busy the restaurant is and the size of the order.

What do the order statuses mean?

Pending means the order is received. Preparing means the kitchen is making it. Ready means you can collect. Done or collected means the order is complete.
